Why work with Philiy?

Philiy Lander’s combination of knowledge & passion for her craft supports teams & leaders to create self-improvement for a better workflow. Understanding & approachable, Philiy enthuses people, helping to break barriers to change to facilitate greater personal & business development. Philiy is a gifted trainer, agilist, facilitator & professional coach, this combination allows her to imbue organisations with new skills but also the ability for real & lasting positive change. As an ICF Associate Certified Coach, ICAgile Authorised Instructor (ICP-ATF, ICP-ACC) & Training From the Back of the Room Trainer, Philiy has the expertise to make a difference amongst the people of an organisation. Further qualifications including Certified Scrum Professional, Certified Professional Agility in the Enterprise & Agility Health Facilitator, mean Philiy brings an overall more holistic skillset to make a difference to you & your organisation.
